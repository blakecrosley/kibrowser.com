A browser that stays out of your way.

Three things, in order. Everything else follows from them.

Block trackers

Built-in content rules block known cross-site trackers and ad networks before they load. No SDK touches your page.

Decide per site

Tap the shield in the address bar to grant or revoke any permission for that origin. Ki remembers your choice.

Stay on device

Bookmarks, history, tabs, and per-site settings stay on your iPhone. Nothing syncs to a server we control.
