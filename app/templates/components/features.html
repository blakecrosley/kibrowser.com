Everything in one app

What you get

Trackers blocked

Built-in content rules block known cross-site trackers and ad networks before they load.

Per-site control

Tap the shield in the address bar to grant or revoke any permission for that origin. Ki remembers your choice.

Privacy profiles

Switch between profiles for research, shopping, or personal use without crossing cookies or history.

Reader mode

Strip the page down to text and images. JavaScript disabled and a locked-down Content-Security-Policy in the reader view.

On-device assistant

Long-press the URL bar and tell Ki what to do. Apple Intelligence on supported iPhones; bring your own Anthropic or OpenAI key otherwise.

On-device only

Bookmarks, history, tabs, and per-site permissions stay on your iPhone. Nothing syncs to a server we control.

No account

Nothing to sign up for. No email collected. No data to sell, because we have none.
